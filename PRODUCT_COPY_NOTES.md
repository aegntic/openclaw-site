# Product Copy Notes

## Positioning

OpenClaw is presented as agent infrastructure that stays legible under pressure.

## Core narrative

- one bloated operator becomes mush
- a calm operator plus narrow specialists scales cleanly
- registry, dispatcher, scheduler, genesis, evaluator, and lifecycle are the key layers
- the website mirrors the organism so the marketing feels structurally true

## Upgrade paths

- replace placeholder pricing with real commercial motion
- add customer stories once they exist
- connect form submissions to CRM or outbound workflow
- add launch videos, architecture diagrams, and benchmark pages
